About the Institution

Sacred Heart Higher Secondary School in Sitapur, Uttar Pradesh, is a co-educational institution affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). Established in 1954, it is managed by the Sacred Heart Catholic Education Society and has a long-standing reputation for academic excellence and character development.
